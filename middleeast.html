We went to the Middle East with some of our best friends in July 2013. We started the trip in Istanbul, Turkey and stayed at the Hilton. We visited a traditional Turkish bath and spent the day at the Blue Mosque. We took a short flight to Cappadocia where we woke up extremely early to take a beautiful hot air balloon ride and ride ATVs through the cave.


After Turkey, we flew to Tel Aviv, Israel and took a bus to Jerusalem. We ate delicious hummus in Old Jerusalem, went to the Western Wall and walked the last steps of Jesus Christ. Unfortunately, we couldn't visit the Muslim Quarters as we were visiting over Ramadan.


We crossed the border from Turkey to Jordan on foot. In Jordan, we stayed in a traditional Bedouin camp. We rode camels through the desert and hiked through Petra over two days.
